Thai kohlrabi and crab salad

Description:

Indulge in a burst of flavors with our Thai Kohlrabi and Crab Salad. This vibrant dish combines the crispness of kohlrabi with the delicate sweetness of crab, all elevated by a zesty Thai-inspired dressing. Each bite offers a harmonious blend of textures and tastes, making it a perfect appetizer or light meal option.

Ingredients

  • 150g white crab meat
  • Â½ clove garlic
  • 2 birds-eye chillies
  • 2 tbsp, plus 1 tbsp roughly chopped to garnish roasted peanuts
  • 1 tbsp soft light brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 125g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 100g green beans, halved
  • 1 large (about 250g) kohlrabi, peeled and match sticked

  1. Remove the crab from the fridge just as you start to assemble the salad, so it is not fridge-cold when served.

  2. Smash the garlic with a large pestle and mortar, then add the chillies and peanuts, and bruise. Add the brown sugar, fish sauce and lime juice, and mix well. Tip in the cherry tomatoes and bruise again. Pour into a large bowl, add the green beans and kohlrabi, and toss well.

  3. Divide between 4 plates and top each salad with the crab and a sprinkling of peanuts.
