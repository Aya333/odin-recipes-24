Pasta Alla Genovese

Description:

Transport your taste buds to the heart of Italy with our exquisite Pasta alla Genovese. This classic dish hails from the vibrant city of Genoa and embodies the rich culinary heritage of the region. At its core, Pasta alla Genovese is a celebration of simplicity, combining tender pasta with a luxurious sauce of slow-cooked onions, fragrant basil, and creamy Parmesan cheese. Each forkful is a symphony of flavors, with the sweet caramelized onions melding beautifully with the aromatic basil and savory Parmesan. Whether enjoyed as a comforting weeknight meal or a sophisticated dinner party dish, our Pasta alla Genovese is sure to delight even the most discerning palates.

Ingredients

  • 300g trofie pasta
  • 150g new potatoes, halved
  • 100g green beans, trimmed
  • 1 small tub fresh pesto
  • parmesan or vegetarian alternative, to serve

  1. Cook the pasta following pack instructions. Cook the potatoes in boiling, salted water until tender, adding the green beans for the last 4 minutes of cooking. Drain the pasta, keeping 2 tbsp of the cooking liquid.

  2. Drain the beans and potatoes then tip into the pasta with the pesto, cooking liquid and a squeeze of lemon. Toss everything together and serve with extra parmesan if you like.
